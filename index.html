About

Hello, World!
My name is Fenty, a Full Stack developer with experience in JavaScript, React, Redux and Ruby on Rails. I have a background in architecture with a focus on healthcare and sustainability discipline. I have had a fascination for building things since I was young, from arts and crafts to assembling built-construction.

In 2020, I found my new passion in programming, and it aligns with my true interest in analytical thinking and problem solving. I began my journey from Codecademy and freeCodeCamp platform to enrolling myself in the Flatiron School Software Engineering program. I believe the core principles of architecture and my background in mathematics will be a good catalyst to my growth in the field of programming. I am excited to contribute my skills and past experiences to this new journey!
